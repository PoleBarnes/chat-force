# BlackTie Post-Frame Buildings — Brand Context

## Company
BlackTie designs and sells post-frame (pole barn) building packages. They provide
engineered building kits — not construction services. Customers buy the package
and either self-build or hire a contractor.

## Market Position
- Regional player in agricultural/commercial post-frame buildings
- Known for quality materials and engineering
- Competitive pricing on engineered packages
- Strong in agricultural market segment

## Target Audiences
1. **Farmers/Ranchers** — Cold storage, equipment storage, livestock shelters
2. **Rural property owners** — Workshops, garages, hobby buildings
3. **Small businesses** — Commercial storage, retail, workshops
4. **Contractors** — Buy packages to build for their clients

## Product Line
- Cold storage buildings (various sizes)
- Engineered building packages (custom plans)
- Commercial buildings
- Agricultural buildings
- Residential outbuildings

## Key Specs (standard across products)
- 29 ga steel exterior
- Laminated posts (2x6 or 2x8 depending on size)
- 4' on center trusses, 8' on center posts
- LVL truss carriers and OHD headers
- Wainscot standard
- 2' overhang standard
- Walk doors with windows
- Slider windows

## Brand Voice
- Down-to-earth, practical
- Speaks the language of builders and farmers
- Emphasizes value and quality materials
- Not flashy — trustworthy and knowledgeable
- Uses industry terminology naturally (OHD, post-frame, laminated posts, truss carrier)
